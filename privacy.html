Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information RAVEN collects, how we use it, and how our SMS program works.

Effective April 4, 2026
Applies to ravensplit.com

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information, including mobile phone numbers, with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We only use your information to operate RAVEN, deliver requested notifications, and support your account.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as your email address, first name, last name, and optional phone number.
  • Bill, receipt, and trip information you create inside RAVEN.
  • Payment handles you choose to store, such as Venmo, Cash App, Zelle, or Apple Pay contact details.
  • Technical information needed to secure and operate the service.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage bills, trips, comments, receipts, and payment reminders.
  • To send transactional emails and transactional SMS messages you request or consent to receive.
  • To provide support, prevent abuse, and maintain service reliability.

SMS Privacy

Program name: RAVEN bill and payment notifications

Message type: Transactional text messages related to bills, trips, payment reminders, payment confirmations, and account activity.

Message frequency: Varies based on your activity and the bills or trips you participate in.

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt out: Reply STOP at any time.

Help: Reply HELP or contact support@ravensplit.com.

Mobile Information Sharing

RAVEN does not share mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share limited mobile information with service providers only as necessary to operate the service, such as Twilio for SMS delivery. These providers may not use that information for their own marketing purposes.

Consent for Text Messaging

By providing your phone number and opting in through our website or application flow, you agree to receive transactional text messages from RAVEN related to bills, payments, and trips you participate in.

Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchase. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Third-Party Service Providers

  • Twilio for SMS delivery
  • Supabase for database and authentication infrastructure
  • Anthropic for receipt parsing features when you upload receipt images

These providers only receive information needed to perform their services for RAVEN.

Data Retention

We keep account and bill-related information for as long as needed to operate your account and maintain your history, unless you request deletion or we need to retain certain records for security or legal reasons.

Your Rights

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us at support@ravensplit.com.
