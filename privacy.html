Privacy Policy

Effective: March 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

RAVEN ("we", "us", "our") is a bill-splitting service that operates via SMS, iMessage, and Discord. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

Information We Collect

When you interact with RAVEN, we may collect:

  • Phone numbers — to send and receive SMS/iMessage communications
  • Message content — the text commands you send (e.g. SPLIT, PAID, REMIND) to process your requests
  • Bill and transaction data — amounts, participant names, and payment statuses you provide
  • Discord user IDs — when you use the RAVEN Discord bot
  • Usage data — command frequency and feature usage to improve the service

We do not collect financial account information, payment credentials, or government IDs.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Process SPLIT, PAID, REMIND, and STATUS commands
  • Deliver bill breakdowns and reminders to your group
  • Maintain bill history and track payment status
  • Improve service reliability and feature quality
  • Respond to support requests

Data Retention

Bill and transaction data is retained for as long as your account is active. Free tier users have access to up to 5 active bills at a time. Pro users retain full bill history.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by texting DELETE to +1 (516) 234-7187 or by contacting us at [email protected].

Third-Party Services

RAVEN uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Twilio — SMS and iMessage delivery
  • Discord API — bot functionality on Discord servers
  • Railway — backend infrastructure and hosting

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit. We do not store message content beyond what is necessary to process your active bills.

Children's Privacy

RAVEN is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify active users via SMS when material changes occur. Continued use of RAVEN after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
