Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started? ↓
Just text SPLIT to +1 (516) 234-7187. No app download, no sign-up, no account needed. Add the number to your group chat and you're ready.
Does RAVEN work on Android? ↓
Yes. RAVEN works over standard SMS, so any phone — iPhone or Android — can text the RAVEN number. The iMessage experience is richest on iPhone-only group chats, but SMS works universally.
Does RAVEN actually send money? ↓
No. RAVEN tracks who owes what and sends reminders — it does not process payments. You still settle up through Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, or cash. RAVEN just removes the awkwardness of asking.
What's the difference between Free and Pro? ↓
The Free plan gives you 1 RAVEN number, up to 5 active bills, and manual reminders. Pro ($9/group/month) unlocks unlimited bills, scheduled auto-reminders, full bill history and export, and custom reminder messages.
Can I use RAVEN with an existing group chat? ↓
Yes — just add RAVEN's number to your existing group chat. Everyone in the chat can interact with it immediately.
Someone in my group isn't getting messages. What should I do? ↓
Make sure they haven't blocked the RAVEN number, and that their carrier supports SMS from short codes or 10DLC numbers. If issues persist, text HELP to +1 (516) 234-7187 or email [email protected].
How do I cancel my Pro subscription? ↓
Text CANCEL to +1 (516) 234-7187 or email [email protected]. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
How do I delete my data? ↓
Text DELETE ACCOUNT to +1 (516) 234-7187. All your bills and associated data will be permanently removed. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
